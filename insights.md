# Customer Segmentation Insights (RFM Analysis)

## Champions (237 customers)
- High recency, frequency, and monetary value
- Represent the most valuable customer group
- **Action:** Offer exclusive rewards, loyalty perks, and early access to products

## Loyal Customers (235 customers)
- Frequent purchasers with moderate spending
- High potential for revenue growth
- **Action:** Introduce bundles, premium memberships, and personalized offers

## Potential Loyalists (17 customers)
- Recent customers with low purchase frequency
- Early-stage relationship with high future potential
- **Action:** Run onboarding campaigns and first-repeat purchase incentives

## At Risk Customers (237 customers)
- Previously active but showing reduced engagement
- High churn probability
- **Action:** Launch re-engagement campaigns and limited-time discounts

## Lost Customers (220 customers)
- Long inactive with low engagement
- Low immediate value
- **Action:** Run low-cost win-back experiments or deprioritize marketing spend
